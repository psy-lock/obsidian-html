Progresion

TEC. TIER 1 (no travel needed)

  • Sylva
    • Key resource: Zinc, Malachite(Copper), Hydrogen, Nitrogen
    • Basic resource: Compound, Resin, Laterite, Clay, Quartz, Graphite, Ammonium, Organic

TEC. TIER 2

  • Desolo/Calidor
    • Key resource: Wolframite
    • This resource is intended to be able to create your first Chemistry Lab.
  • Vesani/Novus 
    • Key resource: Lithium
    • Thanks to Lithium you can start the production of Batteries and in the future RTGs

TEC. TIER 2,5 (Tec Tier 2 recommended but not necessary)

  • Glacio/Novus
    • Key resource: Hematite (Iron)
    • The Iron allows you to get your first Atmospheric Condenser to collect the Gases necessary for the advancement of the Tec. Tier. Other main uses are Extralarge Platform C, Shredders and Large Silos
  • Glacio/Vesania 
    • Key resource: Titanite (Titanium)
    • Necessary to make Titanium Alloy (Tec Tier 2 necessary) and build the Large Shuttle and Hydrazine Jetpack. Pure Titanium is useful for making Medium Storage Silos.

TEC. TIER 3

  • Glacio/Vesania  
    • Key resource: Argon
    • Key Gas to produce Steel using iron and coal. You can create High-Level modules such as Hydrazine Thruster, Extralarge Shredder and Auto Extractors
  • Novus/Atrox 
    • Key resource: Methane
    • Start Silicone production to create the Paver to add to your Large Rover, also necessary for Shelters in case you want to use them.

TEC. TIER 4

  • ATROX 
    • Key resource: Helium
    • RTG, RTG, RTG! Free Energy the ultimate goal. Helium basically serves to produce Nano Carbon Alloy, the union of Titanium Alloy with Steel. You can build Large Canister if you like them.
